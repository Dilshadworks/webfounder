Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Virtual Private Server (VPS)?

A Virtual Private Server is a dedicated hosting environment for your website. Because your site will have its own dedicated environment, you wont share resources with other customers. Not having to share RAM or CPU with other customers means you wont be affected by their sites behavior

What are the benefits of VPS hosting?

Our VPS hosting is fully managed, powerful, and has dedicated resources that improve performance and allow you to scale your business and website instantly and without downtime.

When should I use VPS hosting instead of shared hosting?

If you require more control over your hosting environment, youll want to consider VPS hosting. It gives you the ability to increase CPU, RAM, and disk space without overspending on the things you dont need.

Is VPS faster than shared hosting?

A VPS hosting plan will always be faster than a shared hosting plan. You wont have to compete with other sites and will allow a dedicated stream of traffic to your site at all times.
