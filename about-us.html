The Core Values We Live By

ChemiCloud is Independent

We are proud to be one of the few privately-owned, independent global web hosting companies in the industry. This means that we own 100% of our business, allowing us to be a customer-oriented company and constantly innovate our cloud hosting platform.

ChemiCloud is Easy-to-use

Web hosting doesn’t need to be complicated. That’s why we want to make your hosting experience as simple as possible. And even though we keep it simple, our lab is packed with many design-friendly Panel tools with all the features you need. And if you have a request or new feature, we’re always open to your feedback.

Our Customers Are Our Biggest Asset

We value our customers and do everything possible to ensure their happiness. If it wasn’t for you and all of the amazing customers like you, we wouldn’t have been able to grow over the years. Thanks to customers like you who have shared your experience through word of mouth and customer referrals, we’ve been able to make a difference and stand out from a long list of hundreds of web hosting companies.

ChemiCloud is Transparent

You won’t find any hidden fees in our plans. We don’t believe in offering “unlimited plans” that come with costly upgrades down the road. What you see is what you get. Our terms are always clear and always honest.

ChemiCloud Goes the Extra Mile

We’ve been focusing on providing real value and building a fast and knowledgeable support team. We value our customers and do everything possible to ensure their happiness. No rules or policies can ever provide an amazing customer experience.

Our History Over the Years

2016

Hello, world!

ChemiCloud was founded in 2016 with a desire to create a reliable cloud hosting platform and to offer the best customer experience in the industry, and that's our promise. We don't settle for anything else, and we are here to stay. We're a trusted and passionate team of specialists with a solid background and over ten years of experience exclusively in the hosting industry.

2017

The first 100 Customers

Getting our first 100 Customers was the hardest journey. As of June 2017, ChemiCloud had 100+ active customers. A few months later, there would be 1000+ happy customers.

2018

The first industry awards

Our hard work during the past years was finally well-rewarded - we have been awarded user's top choice on HostAdvice and rated 5 stars on the web hosting community. Furthermore, we rolled out an infrastructure update for our shared hosting plans switching all servers to LiteSpeed web server for top speed performance.

2019

Expanding our data centers locations

In our efforts to be near clients that need us, we've expanded to additional data centers in the United Kingdom, Singapore, and India, offering seven global locations. Because we're constantly evolving, we're always looking to expand to additional areas.

2020

Optimizing our infrastructure

Furthermore, we've continued to find ways to optimize our infrastructure and passing those benefits on to you, our customers. With this in mind, we've boosted the resources allocated across our shared hosting plans to give our customers even more flexibility and performance.

2021

ChemiCloud is growing

From a small team in 2020, we entered 2021 as a distributed team of thirteen+ people. As we’re constantly evolving, we are always hunting for great talent who bears the same values and is as enthusiastic about helping people get online as we are.
